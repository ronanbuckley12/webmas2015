WebMas 2015

17th December; Great causes, 24 hours.


What is WebMas?

Great causes; 24 hours.


Great & innovative sites built for
Charities and Good causes; designed
to support and enhance existing campaigns.

On the 17th December, teams of students
from NCI will spend just 24 hours
designing, developing and deploying sites.
Each team will be assigned
an industry expert as a mentor for the day.

Students get real, hands on
experience, charities get well designed,
future proof websites.
What's not to like?

The Charities

Check out the charities!



Friends Of A
Friends of A is a collection of friends with an aim to raise money to fund ongoing research into cranial aneurysms.


The Croft Community
The Croft Community provides accommodation
for adults with a learning disability in a home
setting.


Blood Bike Leinster
We transport urgently needed blood and other medical requirements between Medical Centers in Leinster.


Humans Too
Humans too is a charity which will tackle homelessness in Dublin
city centre.


Active First Responders
We are a community based group that will be fully trained in dealing with and attending Cardiac Arrests in the local area.


Ceeds Clondalkin
Providing services to local people who want to help improve their employment prospects.


Capuchin Day Centre
The Capuchin Day Centre provides hundreds of breakfast and dinner meals everyday to those who are struggling.
